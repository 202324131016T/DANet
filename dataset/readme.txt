For the her2st dataset, the data is publicly available here: human HER2-positive breast tumor ST data.

https://github.com/almaan/her2st/

Move the her2st dataset to ./dataset/her2st_data/her2st/


For the GSE240429 dataset, the data is publicly available for download at 

https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/geo/query/acc.cgi?acc=GSE240429

Move the GSE240429 dataset (GSE240429_RAW.tar) to ./dataset/GSE240429_data/
